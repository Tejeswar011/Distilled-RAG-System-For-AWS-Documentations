Elastic Beanstalk, on the other hand, is a more flexible service
for deploying and scaling web applications. It supports multiple
programming languages and web servers, automatically handles
capacity provisioning, load balancing, auto-scaling, and
application health monitoring. Elastic Beanstalk integrates
seamlessly with other AWS services like RDS and CloudWatch,
allowing for more complex architectures and fine-grained control
over the underlying resources.
